Project Abstract

The Smart Gas Cylinder Monitoring System is designed to provide real-time tracking of gas levels in cylinders, making it ideal for households, restaurants, and industrial settings. Using precision sensors connected to an IoT-enabled platform, the system continuously measures the weight of the cylinder and calculates the remaining gas. It offers accurate estimated usage times, helping users plan refills efficiently and avoid unexpected shortages. The data is displayed live on a web interface, allowing users to monitor their gas levels from anywhere. This project emphasizes convenience, operational efficiency, and streamlined resource management by leveraging modern IoT technology and real-time data analysis.

What Makes It Smart?

Real-time Gas Monitoring

Constantly tracks the gas cylinder’s weight to ensure you always know how much gas remains.

Estimated Usage Time

Predicts how long the gas will last based on average consumption rates.

Safety Alerts

Alerts you when the cylinder is almost empty or when unusual activity is detected.

Mobile Connectivity

Stay updated from anywhere via IoT integration and notifications.
